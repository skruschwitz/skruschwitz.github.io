Northern Saw-whet Owl

The Northern Saw-Whet Owl's common name comes from the "skiew" call it makes when it is alarmed.

The northern saw-whet owl has a round, light, white face with brown and cream streaks; they also have a dark beak and yellow eyes. They resemble the short-eared owl, because they also lack ear tufts, but are much smaller.
